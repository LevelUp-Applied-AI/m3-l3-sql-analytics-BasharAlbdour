# KPI Brief — Levant Tech Solutions

## KPI 1: Department Salary Efficiency

**Definition:**  
Average salary per employee in each department (Total salary ÷ Number of employees in the department).  

**Current value:**  
- Engineering: 93,800  
- Customer Support: 46,400  
- Finance: 68,833  
- HR: 56,000  
- Marketing: 64,666  
- Operations: 55,500  
- Research: 69,750  
- Sales: 69,666  

**Interpretation:**  
Shows the cost per employee per department. Departments like Engineering have higher costs, indicating either higher skill levels or higher salary scales.

## KPI 3: Employee Utilization Rate

**Definition:**  
Percentage of employees assigned to at least one project.  

**Current value:**  
- Total employees: 60  
- Employees not assigned to any project: 18  
- Utilization rate: 70%  

**Interpretation:**  
70% of employees are actively assigned to projects, highlighting workforce usage. 30% are unassigned and could be allocated to improve productivity.
